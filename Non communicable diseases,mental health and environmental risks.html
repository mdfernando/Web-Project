Non communicable diseases

A non-communicable disease (NCD) is a disease that is not transmissible directly from one person to another.

NCDs include

  • Parkinson's disease
  • Autoimmune diseases
  • Strokes
  • Most heart diseases
  • Most cancers
  • Diabetes
  • Chronic kidney disease
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Osteoporosis
  • Alzheimer's disease
  • Cataracts
